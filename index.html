The Canadian healthcare system can seem confusing
I decided to navigate it through conversation.

Join me as I speak with a wide range of healthcare stakeholders—CEOs, policy experts, tech innovators, clinicians, researchers, and more. Each episode dives into their unique stories and perspectives, helping us all better understand how healthcare really works. Pick an episode that piques your interest, and learn along with me!

Conversations That Clarify

Dive deep into Canada’s healthcare landscape. Each episode breaks down the complex issues, giving you clear insights into how policies, technology, and frontline experiences shape the care we receive.

Stories That Inspire

Meet the people behind the scenes—clinicians, researchers, tech innovators, and policy makers. Their personal journeys and challenges shed light on what truly drives Canadian healthcare forward.

Insights You Can Use

Whether you’re a patient, professional, or simply curious, these conversations arm you with practical knowledge. Learn how health systems operate, how innovations take root, and how you can play a role.
